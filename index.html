Signs you might need a new roof

Not sure if it's time? Watch for these.

If you spot any of these, book a free inspection — catching it early can save you thousands.

  • Missing, cracked, or curling shingles
  • Granules collecting in your gutters
  • Water stains on ceilings or in the attic
  • Daylight visible through the roof boards
  • Sagging rooflines or soft spots
  • Your roof is 15–20+ years old
  • Recent hail or windstorm in your area
  • Higher energy bills from poor insulation

Roofing FAQs

Questions Arkansas homeowners ask us.

Is the roof inspection really free?

Yes — 100% free with no obligation. We inspect your roof, show you photos of what we find, and give you honest recommendations. If your roof is in good shape, we'll happily tell you so.

My roof was hit by a storm. Can you help with insurance?

Absolutely. Storm and hail damage is our specialty. We document everything, meet your adjuster, and advocate on your behalf so the claim process is as stress-free as possible.

Do I need a full replacement or just a repair?

Often a repair is all you need — and we'll always tell you the truth. Our free inspection gives you a clear, photo-backed answer and honest options, never a pushy upsell.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roofs are completed in one to two days, depending on size and weather. We'll give you a clear timeline up front and keep your property clean throughout.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — Rooftop Heroes Roofing is licensed and fully insured, and our workmanship is backed by warranty. We're happy to provide proof before any work begins.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Little Rock, Central Arkansas, and surrounding communities across the state. Not sure if you're in our area? Just give us a call.
